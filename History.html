Visit Lutterworth

This little town in Leicestershire, on the border of both Warwickshire and Northamptonshire, seems like it wouldn't play a major part in the history of both the United Kingdom but also the world.

From the place the first ever translation of the Bible from Latin into English was done.

To the place where Frank Whittle worked on the world's first jet engine.

Sure to a regular person, this small market town this small town might be unheard of, but in history this is a mighty little town.

On this tour you will be shown the history of Lutterworth.
